# Launch Readiness Notes

## Decision and owner
The payment migration launch decision is gated by the release owner and the operations lead.
The owner must confirm that the rollback runbook is reviewed before launch.

## Risks and blockers
The main blocker is an unresolved dependency on the billing export job.
A secondary risk is support-volume growth after the migration announcement.

## Metrics
The review uses three metrics: migration success rate, support ticket volume, and rollback time.
The target is to keep rollback time under 30 minutes during the launch window.
